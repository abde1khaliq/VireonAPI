Everything you need to know about Vireon

VireonAPI is powered by cutting-edge AI models like Gemini, delivering precision at scale. Our moderation engine achieves an average accuracy of 98.7% across text, images, and embedded media. It’s trained on diverse, real-world datasets and continuously updated to adapt to evolving threats—from slang and context-sensitive abuse to AI-generated content. You get high recall, low false positives, and real-time filtering—without the guesswork.

Our text moderation currently supports 12 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Russian, Arabic, Hindi, Japanese, and Chinese. We're continuously adding more languages based on customer demand.

Our average API response time is under 200ms for text moderation and under 500ms for image moderation.

We take data privacy seriously. All API requests are encrypted in transit, and we don't store your content longer than necessary to process it.
